# Code of Conduct – Hviktor

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Hviktor project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, or level of experience.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be kind, respectful, and constructive in all interactions.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started.
- Accept feedback gracefully and offer it thoughtfully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Deliberately disruptive or hostile conduct.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please reach out to the project maintainers by contacting the
team directly. All reports will be handled with discretion.

Maintainers will address issues on a case-by-case basis with a preference for conversation and resolution over
punishment.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public
representation of the project.
